UI Design Principles

Progressive Disclosure

Critical information displayed prominently with additional detail available through natural interaction. Users see summaries first and drill down only when needed.

Contextual Relevance

Information and actions appropriate to the operator's current focus. When examining a vessel, irrelevant data is hidden to reduce cognitive load.

Minimal Interruption

Alerts calibrated to balance timely information against distraction. System learns from operator responses to refine attention thresholds.
